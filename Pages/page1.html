Beginner Guide

Setptember 21, 2023

Want to learn how to fish? Here is a quick step-to-step guide that I recommend to start your fishing adventures.

Step 1: Buying Fishing Gear
Before going out on the water, you need to buy fishing gear. All you need is a a fishing rod and a reel, the best part is that both could be bought together as a combo deal for about 20 bucks!
Also buy a spool of monofilament fishing line (6- or 8-pound test), a couple of split shot sinkers (BB size) and some hooks (size 2 for small bait, up to size 3/0 for bigger bait.)

Step 2: Getting Bait
crickets, redworms, night crawlers, and minnows can be bought as well in bait shops. But if you want to fun experience and add to your own adventure, try CATCHING YOUR OWN! Try looking under stream rocks, logs, or leaf litters to find worms and crayfish. Dip nets could be used to catch minnows in creeks and streams.
